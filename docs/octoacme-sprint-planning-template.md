## Agenda (timebox: 60–90 min for a 2-week sprint)

| Time | Topic | Owner |
|------|-------|-------|
| 0:00 – 0:05 | Welcome & logistics | PM |
| 0:05 – 0:15 | Review previous sprint outcomes and carry-overs | PM |
| 0:15 – 0:30 | Product Lead presents sprint goal and top backlog items | PdM |
| 0:30 – 0:55 | Team reviews, estimates, and accepts items into sprint | Whole team |
| 0:55 – 1:05 | Identify dependencies, risks, and open questions | PM |
| 1:05 – 1:10 | Confirm sprint commitment and goal statement | PM + PdM |
| 1:10 – 1:15 | Wrap-up and next steps | PM |

---

## Pre-Meeting Checklist (PM / PdM complete before the session)

- [ ] Backlog is groomed and top items have clear acceptance criteria
- [ ] Team capacity calculated (available person-days minus PTO and ceremonies)
- [ ] Dependencies from other teams identified and noted in relevant backlog items
- [ ] Risk register reviewed; new risks added before sprint starts
- [ ] Previous sprint retrospective action items reviewed for carry-forward
