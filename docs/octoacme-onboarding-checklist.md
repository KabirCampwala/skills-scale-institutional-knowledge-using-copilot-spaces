# OctoAcme — New Team Member Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
Help new team members get up to speed quickly and consistently so they can contribute with confidence from day one.

## Who completes this checklist
- The **new team member** works through the checklist during their first two weeks.
- The **onboarding buddy** (a senior teammate) provides support, answers questions, and confirms completion of each section.
- The **Project Manager** tracks overall onboarding progress and removes blockers.

---

## Week 1: Orientation and Access

### Accounts & Tooling
- [ ] GitHub account linked to the organization and added to the relevant team(s)
- [ ] Project board access confirmed (GitHub Projects or equivalent)
- [ ] CI/CD pipeline access and credentials set up
- [ ] Development environment set up and a smoke-test build passing locally
- [ ] Communication tools configured (Slack/Teams channels, email lists, calendar invites)

### Context & Documentation
- [ ] Read the [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read the [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — identify your own role
- [ ] Read the [Project Initiation Guide](octoacme-project-initiation.md)
- [ ] Review the current project charter / one-pager for active projects
- [ ] Review the [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md) guide
- [ ] Review the [RACI Matrix](octoacme-raci-matrix.md) and confirm your responsibilities are clear

### Team Introduction
- [ ] Attended (or scheduled) 1-on-1 intro meetings with PM, PdM, and onboarding buddy
- [ ] Introduced to the broader team during standup or team sync
- [ ] Added to recurring meeting invites (daily standup, weekly delivery sync, sprint planning)

---

## Week 2: Process Immersion and First Contribution

### Delivery Process
- [ ] Read the [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md) guide
- [ ] Read the [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) guide
- [ ] Attended at least one sprint planning or backlog refinement session
- [ ] Attended at least three daily standups
- [ ] Read the [Release & Deployment Guide](octoacme-release-and-deployment.md)

### Quality & Contribution Standards
- [ ] Reviewed the PR workflow expectations (small PRs, CI requirements, approval policy)
- [ ] Opened and merged (or reviewed) at least one pull request following the team's standards
- [ ] Confirmed test coverage expectations and ran the test suite locally

### Retrospective & Improvement Culture
- [ ] Read the [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md) guide
- [ ] Attended or reviewed notes from the most recent retrospective
- [ ] Identified at least one question or improvement idea to raise in the next retro
